Join the Team at Harrison's Hope

At Harrison's Hope, we support people and families living with FASD (Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorder). We believe that no one should have to walk this path alone. By giving your time, you help us build a community where everyone belongs.

How You Can Make a Difference

We have many ways for you to get involved and help our mission grow:

  • Event Support: Help us plan and run our community walks and family fun days
  • Support Groups: Assist our leaders in creating a kind and safe space for families to meet
  • Office Help: Use your skills to help us share news and stay organized
  • Awareness: Help us teach others about FASD so our community can be more helpful and fair

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • Professional Growth: Gain experience in the non-profit sector and build new skills
  • Community Connection: Meet dedicated people who care about making a positive change
  • Lasting Impact: Your work directly helps families find the resources and friendship they need

"Together, we can build a future full of hope and understanding."

We are looking for kind, reliable people to join our team. Whether you can help for one hour or one day a week, we would love to have you.

Support the Mission of Harrison's Hope

At Harrison's Hope, we help families living with FASD. We want every person to feel supported and strong. To do this, we need to raise money for our programs. Your help with fundraising ensures that no family has to face their journey alone.

Ways You Can Help Raise Funds

  • Host an Event: You can organize a bake sale, a car wash, or a fun run with your friends
  • Online Giving: Share our page so your family and friends can donate
  • Corporate Gifts: Ask a local business to support our mission with a gift
  • Special Projects: Help us collect items for our community auctions and raffles

Why Your Help Matters

  • Provide Resources: The money you raise pays for helpful tools and classes for parents
  • Create Fun Memories: Donations help us host parties and events for kids and families
  • Spread Hope: Every dollar helps us tell more people about FASD and how to be kind

"When we work together, we can change lives and build a brighter future."

Raising money is a powerful way to show you care. Whether you raise a little or a lot, every bit makes a big difference for Harrison's Hope.
