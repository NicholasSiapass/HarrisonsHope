FASD-Informed Services & Supports

Harrison's Hope provides a continuum of services for individuals and families living with Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorder (FASD). Our programs are designed with lived experience at the center, ensuring empathetic, effective support that promotes dignity and inclusion.

Caregiver Supports

We provide comprehensive supports for caregivers navigating the complexities of FASD. Our services are designed to reduce isolation, build skills, and create lasting community connections.

Service Navigation

Supporting caregivers through the understanding of services and supports available in their communities, and from other professionals.

Peer to Peer Caregiver Support Programs

Free Twice Weekly Virtual Caregiver Support Groups moderated by experienced fellow caregivers:

  • Every Tuesday Evening (8-9pm EST) - Harrison's Hope FASD Caregiver Support Group
  • Every Thursday Afternoon (1-2pm EST) - Harrison's Hope FASD Caregiver Support Group

Virtual Special Guest Speaker Series

Free Monthly Virtual Guest Speakers share their area of expertise in topics that support individuals living with FASD and their caregivers.

Respite Programs

8-week programs where individuals living with FASD develop life skills and social skills, fully supported by FASD-informed staff, while enjoying time in their communities with others. This provides committed time for caregivers to rest, recover, and rejuvenate.

See our Calendar of Events for current respite program offerings.

Caregiver Workshops & Conferences

Customized workshops and conferences with content developed for caregivers and their loved ones, covering topics spanning the lifespan. Latest research and data-driven information offers families a path forward for today and for the future.

Services for Agencies, Organizations, Schools & Institutions

We offer consultation, collaboration, and training to service providers, programs, organizations, and schools seeking to become FASD-informed.

Consultations, Collaborations, Partnerships & Committee Advisories

Consultation and collaboration is available to service providers, programs, organizations and schools. We have provided consultations to:

  • Various groups and school boards
  • Not-for-profits and foster/adoption agencies
  • Volunteer organizations and more

Harrison's Hope Customized Training Offerings

We offer three levels of training packages to accommodate the length of time available, number of delegates, context and learning aspirations for service providers, agencies, and institutions seeking FASD knowledge growth.

Plenary Speech / Workshop

20 minutes - 2 hours

Deliver a keynote or workshop centring on the values of FASD-informed communication, co-production, and collaborative working to enhance understanding and support for individuals affected by FASD.

From $950 CAD

Past venues include:

  • International and national conferences (FASD Success Caregiver Conference, FASD United International FASD Conference)
  • Mental Health & Wellness Events

Team Training

Half - One Day

As part of a conference or team training day, our courses nurture FASD-informed empathic working by creating a shared language and understanding of different perspectives related to FASD. With time to reflect, an action plan is created and personalized report provided.

From $2,800 CAD
