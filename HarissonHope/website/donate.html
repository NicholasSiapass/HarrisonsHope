Ways to Give to Harrison's Hope

There are special ways you can send a gift to help our families. Both ways help us make sure no one has to walk their journey alone.

Monthly Giving: The Best Way to Help

A monthly gift is a small amount of money you send every month. It's like a subscription to kindness!

How it helps: It gives Harrison's Hope a steady hand. We can plan more fun events and buy more tools because we know your help is coming.

Why it's great: You can give a small amount, like $10 or $20, and it adds up to a huge help by the end of the year!

Set Up Monthly Giving

Tribute Giving: A Gift in Someone's Name

A tribute gift is a way to honour someone special. You can give a gift to celebrate a happy day or to remember someone you love.

In Celebration: Give a gift for a birthday, a wedding, or a holiday. It's a wonderful way to say "I care about you!"

In Memory: Give a gift to remember a loved one who has passed away. It turns a sad time into a way to help others.

How it helps: We will send a special card to let the person (or their family) know that a kind gift was made in their name.

Your Donation Makes an Impact

Your kindness helps us provide important services for families. Here is how your gift helps:

Family Support

Your money helps us run groups where parents can meet and support each other, reducing the isolation that too many FASD caregivers face.

Learning Tools, Programs & Services

Donations help us create inclusive resources and programs that educate, reduce stigma, and support sustainable communities.

Community Events

Your donation funds family activities and events that create fun memories and build lasting community connections.

Safe Spaces

Donations help us keep our doors open so families always have a place to go for help, support, and understanding.

Why Your Gift Matters

  • You Provide Hope: Your support shows families that they are not alone.
  • You Create Change: Every dollar helps us build a community that is kind and fair to everyone.
  • You Build a Future: Your contribution can make a tangible difference in the lives of local families by providing critical services and hope.
