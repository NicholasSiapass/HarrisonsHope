Our Mission

Harrison's Hope is founded on the principle of lifelong support for individuals and families living with Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorder (FASD). We recognize the need for a continuum of services that starts with prevention education and extends to senior care, fostering inclusion, dignity, and interdependence.

About Harrison's Hope

Founded on May 6, 2024, Harrison's Hope is a Canadian charity dedicated to transforming the lives of individuals and families living with Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorder (FASD).

Our Founder and Executive Director, Mary Byrnes, leads a disability-led, caregiver- and lived-experience-informed organization committed to creating sustainable, community-based supports across the lifespan.
